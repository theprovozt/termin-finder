# Competitor Research - German Appointment Booking

## Overview
Research done on existing competitors in the German appointment booking aggregation space.

## Competitors Found

### 1. Telegram Bots (Various)
**What they do:** Notification bots that alert when appointments open
- @TerminBot
- @AppointmentDeBot
- Various others

**Coverage:** Single categories (usually one: citizenship OR language OR KFZ)

**How they work:** 
- Scrape or poll government sites
- Send Telegram notifications when slots appear

**Weaknesses:**
- Only notifications, no search/browse
- Single category
- No UI, just bot commands
- Often unreliable

---

### 2. Make it in Germany (make-it-in-germany.com)
**What they do:** Official government portal for immigration

**Coverage:** Information only, redirects to actual booking systems

**How they work:** Static content, links to official systems

**Weaknesses:**
- Not an aggregator
- No appointment search
- Just information portal

---

### 3. IQB (Institut für Qualitätsentwicklung)
**What they do:** TestDaF and other standardized tests

**Coverage:** Language exams only

**How they work:** Official booking system

**Weaknesses:**
- Only TestDaF
- No aggregation
- Limited to test dates

---

### 4. Language School Booking Sites
**What they do:** Individual language schools (Sprachcaffe, etc.)

**Coverage:** One school or network

**How they work:** Own booking systems

**Weaknesses:**
- Single school/network
- No comparison
- No aggregation across providers

---

### 5. Municipal Booking Systems
**What they do:** Individual cities' Bürgeramt, Kfz, etc.

**Coverage:** One city

**How they work:** Each city has its own system

**Weaknesses:**
- Fragmented by city
- No cross-city search
- Many different systems

---

## Key Insight
**No comprehensive aggregator exists.** Most solutions are:
- Single-category (just language OR citizenship)
- Local (one city)
- Notification bots only
- Information portals without search

## Opportunity for TerminPro
First-mover advantage to be the comprehensive aggregator covering:
- All appointment types
- All major cities
- Search + Map + Filters
- Real-time availability
- Notifications (optional)

## Notes
- Government sites often prohibit scraping - legal considerations
- Many use complex booking platforms (Termin24, etc.)
- Some have APIs, most don't
